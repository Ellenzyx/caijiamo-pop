CAIJIAMO POP / 菜夹馍消消乐

CAIJIAMO POP 是一个为手机优先设计的网页小游戏，点开链接即可玩，无需下载。

在 7×7 的菜品棋盘上，点击选中相邻的同类菜，三连即可做出一份菜夹馍；每局 60 秒，右上角的时间条会在最后 10 秒变红并抖动提醒。

  • MY CAIJIAMO：每完成若干三连，会在下方堆叠出不同阶段的菜夹馍图片。
  • 菜单（MENU）：展示所有菜品。每玩完一局会解锁两道新菜，已解锁菜品可以查看中英文做法，未解锁菜品会以灰色显示且无法查看做法，只能收藏。
  • 日历（CALENDAR）：
    - MONTH：点某一天可以标记“这天吃过菜夹馍”，显示为小 caijiamo1 图标。
    - YEAR：按月统计一年中吃菜夹馍的频率，用数码风条形图展示，并可切换年份。

如何在本地运行

  1. 确保已安装 Python 3。
  2. 在项目根目录下启动本地服务器：
cd CAIJIAMO_POP
python3 -m http.server 5173
  1. 在电脑浏览器打开 http://localhost:5173/。
  2. 手机与电脑在同一 Wi‑Fi 下，手机浏览器访问 http://你的电脑IP:5173/。

CAIJIAMO POP

CAIJIAMO POP is a mobile‑first web puzzle game. You can play it directly in the browser without installing anything.

On a 7×7 grid of dishes, tap adjacent tiles of the same type; matching three cooks one CAIJIAMO. Each round lasts 60 seconds, and the time bar turns red and shakes during the last 10 seconds.

  • MY CAIJIAMO: Completed matches stack different CAIJIAMO stages in the lower area.
  • MENU: Shows all dishes. Each finished round unlocks two new dishes. Unlocked dishes are colorful and link to Chinese/English recipes; locked ones are gray and cannot be opened (but can be favorited).
  • CALENDAR:
    - MONTH view: tap a day to toggle a small caijiamo1 icon, marking “ate CAIJIAMO on this day”.
    - YEAR view: shows, per month, how many days CAIJIAMO was eaten, using a retro‑style bar chart, and you can move across years.

Running locally

  1. Make sure Python 3 is installed.
  2. From the project root, start a simple static server:
cd CAIJIAMO_POP
python3 -m http.server 5173
  1. Open http://localhost:5173/ in your desktop browser.
  2. With your phone on the same Wi‑Fi, visit http://<your-computer-ip>:5173/ in the mobile browser.
